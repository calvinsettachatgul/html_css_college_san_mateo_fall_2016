Q. Design a form for flight booking. It should contain the following fields:

a. Type of trip (One way, round trip etc.). This should be a drop down list or radio buttons.

b. Source and destination input fields.

c. Departure and return dates.

d. Other relevant information, such as, class, number of adults or children etc.

e. A submit button.
